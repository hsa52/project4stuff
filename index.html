Greasers and Mushrooms

Instructions

The mushrooms are back and badder than ever. They've stolen some of your essential greaser items.

But they've also left around some coins to pick up. Just for fun.

Avoid the mushrooms by jumping over them, collect the coins, get back your greaser items, and
defeat the Big Bad Mushroom at the end.

Use A and D to move left and right and use W to do a jump.

Avoid the mushrooms because if you hit them then it's game over.

Refresh to try again.

NOTE: You cannot hold W or press it more than once while jumping over a mushroom.
This will cause the game to crash.

P.S. Not all mushrooms are bad(but don't test that on every mushroom)
